I am a PhD candidate at the University of Warwick, supervised by Dr. Hongkai Wen. I'm also a member of the Warwick AI-Systems Lab, which works closely with the Embedded AI team at SAIC (Samsung AI Cambridge).

My research interests include:

  • Neural Architecture Search (and AutoML more broadly)

  • Robust, Interpretable, and Efficent Architecture Design

  • Foundation Models

  • Governance and Political Theory of AI

  • Computational Neuroscience

In 2023, I received [Best Paper] at the International Conference of Automated Machine Learning. Previously, I received a First Class (85%) BSc in Mathematics from the University of Warwick, achieving a first in all 25 assessed modules. My overarching interests are in understanding and producing intelligent systems that can be harnessed for the benefit of humanity.
