I am a PhD candidate at the University of Warwick, focusing on Automated Machine Learning (AutoML), supervised by Dr Hongkai Wen. I'm also a member of the Warwick AI-Systems Lab, which works closely with the Embedded AI team at SAIC (Samsing AI Cambridge). My research interests include:

  • Neural Architecture Search (and AutoML more broadly)

  • Robust and Efficent Architecture Design

  • Foundation Models

  • Computational Neuroscience

  • Political Theory of Artificial Intelligence

Received "Best Paper" at the Internation Conference of Automated Machine Learning 2023. Previously, I received a First Class (85%) BSc in Mathematics from the University of Warwick, achieving a first in all 25 assessed modules. My overarching interests are in understanding and producing intelligent systems that can be harnessed for the good of humanity.
